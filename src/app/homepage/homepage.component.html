#DevFestHighlights

Organizer

Come join in the fun with hundreds of tech enthusiasts and business professionals in our annual most epic dev and biz conference up north. In this one day conference, we cover five tracks filled with over 15 talks and workshops by speakers from local and abroad. Due of last year of amazing support from all you guy we decide to maintain the awesomeness which mean we are continue bringing the 2 biggest flagship events powered by Google to you!

DevFest is a large community organised annual event with sessions & code labs for developers, students, startups and anyone interested to learn the latest trends and technical knowledge of Google platform, product APIs & open source technologies.

The organizing team spent delicate effort to bring to you speakers from abroad and locally, with more than 20 sessions to ensure maximum learning and fun!

Knowledge, skills, fun & swagsss, all in one day. Can you even resist it?

What is GDG & WTM
